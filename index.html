OUR STORY

Valencia is all about creating comfort, great conversations, and providing magical moments for our guests. Our chef-inspired menu, got you covered for all Craft Coffee & Beer lovers. We also serve a chef-inspired menu with all the flavors you find in India. Valencia is the "Locals Living Room" where people come to EAT-DRINK-GATHER!

BREAKFAST
‘Breakfast is a meal apart’. Ours is served from an extensive menu, from 7am on a weekday and a more leisurely 8am at the weekend.
LUNCH & DINNER
For lunch and dinner we offer the choice of our à la carte or all-day menus, both inspired by the grand cafés of Europe.
AFTERNOON TEA
We serve a full Afternoon Tea every day of the week, offering a varied selection of homemade cakes, scones, pastries and coupes.
